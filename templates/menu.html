Tabbouleh

Tabbouleh is made of fresh parsley and mint, a handful of bulgur, finely chopped tomatoes, and onions, with a drizzle of lemon juice and olive oil.

Fattoush

Fattoush is a salad of lettuce, mint, cucumbers, tomatoes, radishes, and fried pita bread or pita chips.

Knefe is a sweet dessert, made of thin noodle shaped pastry mixed with semolina dough.

The dish is made of semolina and cheese dough, filled with Ashta cream. It is either served rolled as small rolls or in small rope-like shapes, covered with Ashta.
